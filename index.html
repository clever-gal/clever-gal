A little bit about me.

For as long as I can remember, I've longed to find my purpose - that big, grand Thing That I'm Meant To Do. I worked several odd jobs over the years, but none that felt like a calling, that truly lit me up, challenged, and inspired me. I knew I loved to write, but didn't feel fulfilled marketing my own pieces. Instead, what I really loved was taking someone else's ideas, concept, or story, and helping them find a voice.

I like to think of myself as a translator; with a background in both copywriting and web development, I'm uniquely positioned to take your message - your world-changing idea - and give it a clear, well-crafted, head-turning spin. I'm here to help you find the perfect words and code to get your message out there, because your message has the power to change the world.
